Artist Statement

Identity and relationships under the critical magnifying glass of societal expectations and the modern age of technology are two monumental subjects that are intimately familiar to me. I'm nonbinary, I'm a lesbian, I'm a mixed race Chinese American. No matter how much I may want to be seen simply as myself, these aspects are glowing neon labels dictating the path of my life.

I also just wanna be able to give other people like myself a respite from society. Come look at this self-indulgent, unabashedly trashy and tasteless art. And I describe it as such with a great amount of pride. *Let's get sillay.*
